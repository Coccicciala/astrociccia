Orion Nebula

The Orion Nebula (M42) is one of the brightest nebulae visible to the naked eye in the night sky. Located in the Milky Way, it is about 1,344 light-years away and is a stellar nursery, where new stars are being born. This image captures the intricate details of the nebula, showcasing its rich colors and structures formed by interstellar gas and dust.
